Growth

Other World's Genetics and Medical Hemp are grown in Fresno CA. Fresno is the perfect place for cultivating Medical hemp due to the Mediterranean climate, no freeze, and plenty of sunshine. Other World's fiber hemp is grown in Nebraska. Other World specializes in contract farming for extraction labs, smoke able flower, and fiber. Harvesting

Our zero waste philosophy and full plant utilization takes place during our harvest. Other World uses every aspect of our hemp plants to create multiple revenue streams.
