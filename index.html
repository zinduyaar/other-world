Bio-Products

Other World uses our remaining medical stalks and stems in conjunction with farming specific fiber varieties to create 100% biodegradable products without using harmful petro-based polymers. OWM's HempStarch™ Sustainable Starch process is a transformational, molecularly modified, starch-based Patent protected technology, which creates products that are marine degradable, compostable and sustainable. Our products degrade quickly, eliminating any concern of them polluting our waterways. OWM's HempStarch technology was developed by Dr. Gordon Moore, founder of Intel, and Dr. Hans Franke, chairman of the board of Chevron Europe.
